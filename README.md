ClinicFlow is an iOS application designed to simplify outpatient clinic visits for patients and caregivers. It addresses common pain points such as uncertain waiting times, difficulty locating departments, and confusion around the sequence of clinic steps.

The app provides real-time queue tracking, indoor clinic navigation, appointment booking, laboratory and pharmacy management, integrated payments, and a Companion Mode for caregivers managing visits on behalf of a dependent. A visit checklist and push notifications keep patients informed at every stage of their visit.
